History Of Fnatic

Fnatic is a professional esports organization headquartered in London, United Kingdom. Founded 23 July 2004, the team has players from all around the world, accross a variety of games such as League Of Legends, Counter-Strike Global Offensive and Dota 2.

Fnatic's League of Legends team won the first ever League of Legends World Championship in 2011, and also holds the record for the most League of Legends Championship Series split titles, totalling five of the seven played so far.

In the 2015 EU LCS Summer Split they became the first LCS team to finish a split undefeated. Because of their accomplishments Fnatic's LoL team is usually considered the best western team in history.
